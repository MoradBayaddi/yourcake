FAQ's Related to Mothers Day Cakes

Q 1. Can I get a personalized cake for mom?

Ans. Absolutely! At Bakingo, you can find attractive custom-made desserts of your choice for mom. Our collection contains a photo and poster cakes along with various other picture customizations available for regular cakes.

Q 2. Do you provide Mother’s Day cake delivery on a specific date and time?

Ans. Specific time delivery is our specialty here at Bakingo. Our wide variety of desserts and cakes are available for delivery all around the year at the date and time slot of our customer’s choosing. The same service is available for Mother’s Day as well! Just pick the date and time slot of delivery and our team will ensure that you receive your dessert on time!

Q 3. Do you offer Midnight Delivery of cakes for Mother’s Day?

Ans. Yes, in fact, our midnight doorstep delivery services are available for our customers year-round. For special dessert customizations on occasions like Mother’s Day, place your midnight delivery order at least 2 to 3 hours ahead so that we can serve you better while following all personalization requests.

Q 4. Which cake is best for Mother's Day?

Ans. The best type of cake for Mother’s Day are customized photo and poster cakes. Other desserts which are popular among our customers are pinata cakes, pull-me-up cakes, photo pulling cakes, etc. Place an order for a dessert that pays tribute to your wonderful mother!

Q 5. Which cake flavor is most popular for Mothers Day?

Ans. Among all of our best-selling cake flavors, regular flavors like chocolate, vanilla, pineapple, strawberry, etc. are mostly preferred by our customers on the occasion of Mother’s Day. Bakingo would also recommend some newly launched creative cakes with a traditional twist such as Gulkand cakes, Gulaab Jamun cakes, and Rasmalai cakes.
